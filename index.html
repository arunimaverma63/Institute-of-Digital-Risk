About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk. We bridge the gap between academic insight from UK university partnerships and real-world industry projects, equipping professionals with the skills needed for roles in regulated sectors like financial services and critical infrastructure.

Our mission is to foster a community of resilient risk practitioners who can navigate the complexities of emerging technologies, including AI governance and cybersecurity frameworks.

Our Service Pillars

Academy

Comprehensive training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals, covering digital risk fundamentals, cyber threats, and compliance standards.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, future risk models, and AI governance frameworks through collaborative research and prototyping.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement and adapt frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2 for robust digital risk management.

Our Community

IDR serves a diverse community including students, early-career professionals, and experienced practitioners in sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure. We provide upskilling opportunities in digital and cyber risk, enabling career advancement and organizational resilience in an increasingly digital world.
