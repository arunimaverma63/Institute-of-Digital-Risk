Who We Are

Industry-Led,
Academic-Backed

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a specialised training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

By partnering with leading UK universities, IDR ensures practitioners are equipped with both rigorous academic foundations and the practical agility required to manage complex risk landscapes in real-world projects. Our model closes the gap between classroom theory and operational delivery.

We develop professionals who can navigate the intersection of AI governance, cybersecurity frameworks, and enterprise risk — ready to make an impact from day one.

Our Pipeline

  1. 01

    Train

    Intensive bootcamps and structured learning with university-accredited pathways.

  2. 02

    Hire

    Direct placement with partner organisations in financial services and critical infrastructure.

  3. 03

    Innovate

    Developing proprietary IP, AI governance frameworks, and future-state risk models.

  4. 04

    Deploy

    Practitioners embedded in high-consequence environments, delivering real-world impact.
